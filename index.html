Carbon Footprint Manager

Welcome to the Carbon Footprint Manager, your tool for measuring and understanding your environmental impact. Our software empowers you to calculate your carbon footprint by inputting your daily activities and then compares it to global data. Take a step towards a greener future with us.

Getting Started

To get started, follow these steps:

  1. Click the "Register Now" link below the "Register/ Login Button"

  2. Upon successful registration, you should now be able to log in using your registered account.

  3. Upon successful login, you will see the main screen. There, you can select from the list of categories on the left and add activities to the list

  4. Fill out the details on the input, press submit and you will see your input on the card below. Right after that, a real time assessment will pop up on the right of the screen showing you your carbon footprint analysis and comparison among the general average of countries (emmission-wise) globally.

  5. You can stack multiple activities together. The results on the right will base on all of your inputs accross all categories.

  6. Once you are done using the application, you can click the "Sign Out" button on the left side of the screen. A prompt will appear asking you to confirm your sign out.Regarding signouts, the app implements a single sign in policy. If you try to login using the same account on a different instance of the application, the older app will be automatically signed out.
